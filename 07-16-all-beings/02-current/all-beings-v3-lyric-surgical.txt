LYRICS
================================================================================

[Intro: murmured, close dry mic, nylon guitar two bars + air]
Ears up. Fox face. Dirty gold.
[two bars air]

[Verse 1: matter-of-fact chest voice, picked nylon, walking — fox meet]
I touch the middle of his skull — both ears fold down.
He stares a beat, then runs like the ground is news.
Soft under the grime, a twitch mid-step —
every next patch of dust still chosen by him.
He sits, he rolls — ruins the clean I wiped.
Not mine. Not anyone's. Still he finds me.

[Chorus: same four lines every time — unhurried, almost spoken into pitch]
Some pets are only things.
A rope draws a circle — and that is the world.
The door goes numb. The dog goes free.
I don't rescue it. I learn to look.
[two-bar strip]

[Verse 2: slightly denser, light upright bass — garage]
Black garage — dust smell before you see him.
A hole chewed under the door where longing learned to dig.
Door locked: eyes go numb — worse than fear.
Door open: he bolts like hunger learned to run.
What did they buy him for — to manage, to walk?
I slide bread through the dark and leave.

[Chorus]
Some pets are only things.
A rope draws a circle — and that is the world.
The door goes numb. The dog goes free.
I don't rescue it. I learn to look.

[Bridge: near-spoken parade, one string + room — 众人]
"Don't raise that kind of thing."
"Look. Don't touch."
"That dead-lame dog — walking like that —"
I stare one second too long.
I decide not to get hurt.
And the dog? He can't hear the words.
[hang]
"Is he yours?" — fake smile. I say no. I walk.
Then she curses a body that can't talk back.
"You fear he harms you — he will."

[Verse 3: open air, light lift under — two motion frames + pure exchange spark]
Under the bridge he stands on two legs, looking for me —
then hits the ground and runs.
Girl on the ramp opens both arms.
He jumps the whole slope like the air owes him.
With him it's simple — heat for heat, no interest in the kindness.
Kids pour around him. Some people live thinner than the dog.

[Final Chorus: same spell; line 4 micro-vary only; optional thin pad]
Some pets are only things.
A rope draws a circle — and that is the world.
The door goes numb. The dog goes free.
I don't rescue it. I learn to look —
still not mine. Still free.

[Outro: whisper dry, guitar only]
I crouch. I watch.
I want to paint that frame —
and leave the rest unsolved.
[End]
