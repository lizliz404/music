## 1. Song profile

**Title:** Rope Radius  
**BPM / key lean:** 94 BPM · Am (modal, drifts A Dorian on chorus lift)  
**Length:** ~3:12  
**Axis:** Field-notebook witness song — two dogs, many mouths, one way of looking that ends wanting to paint a frame, not fix a life.  
**Hook lines:**  
- *Some pets are only things*  
- *Rope circle, dust and door*  
- *Want to paint that frame — leave the rest unresolved*  
**Arrangement note:** Acoustic spine, brushed percussion entering verse 2, bridge strips to voice + single plucked string, final section widens with harmonium or bowed pad then exhales to near-silence. Rests are structural — leave bars empty after chorus 1 and before the ramp scene.

## 3. Three distinct style containers

**Container A — Field Folk**  
**Style of Music:** intimate indie folk, 94 BPM, fingerpicked acoustic guitar, brushed snare, close warm female vocal, field-recording air, Am modal, sparse intro, dynamic swell on final section  
**Exclude:** EDM, trap beats, heavy distortion, choir, auto-tune, epic orchestral, cute ukulele jingle, anthemic pop belt

**Container B — Grey Chamber**  
**Style of Music:** minimalist chamber folk, 92 BPM, cello drone, soft piano chords, subtle tape hiss, restrained male-female duet optional on chorus only, cinematic silence between phrases, dark garage verses, luminous final lift  
**Exclude:** rock drums, synth pop, country twang, rap, metal, bright commercial pop, children's music, gospel swell, four-on-the-floor dance

**Container C — Urban Observation**  
**Style of Music:** art-pop folk, 96 BPM, muted electric guitar harmonics, lo-fi kick and rimshot, mellotron pad on chorus, spoken-word bridge over single plucked note, dry upfront vocal, bittersweet unresolved outro fade  
**Exclude:** heavy bass drop, reggae, punk, opera, tropical house, metal screaming, glossy radio production, motivational build, happy whistle hook
