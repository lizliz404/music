# "Next Step"

**Suggested Title:** "Next Step"

**Creative Rationale:** Woven from the real melody seed recorded walking to Shenzhen North Station, these lyrics pull the source material's most vivid images — the cement mixer's bone-deep resonance, screen-lit faces at the station, a family dinner table unchanged for a decade, the street stall frozen in time — into a unified transit metaphor of trains, platforms, and luggage. The song traces the arc from suffocation in a PTSD-inducing rental through the relief and disorientation of departure, arriving at the hard-won honesty of walking forward without a map. Every chorus returns to "I don't even know" as both confession and forward momentum — not despair, but clear-eyed movement.

**BPM/Key:** 92 BPM, D minor

---

I lay there like I'd already lost and won

I zipped my bag, I didn't make a sound
