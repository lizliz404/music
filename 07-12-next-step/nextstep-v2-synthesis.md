# "Next Step" — v2 Synthesis

**Title:** Next Step
**BPM/Key:** 92 BPM, D minor
**Creative Rationale:** Fusing the strongest images from both v1 takes — Cursor's visceral opening ("cement truck rolling through my ribs") and tighter rhythmic V2 ("same black pan, same arches"), Hermes' cleaner Pre-Chorus and the bridge question about the weight of standing still. The corrected ASR confirms "I don't even know where's next step" as the exclusive hook, with "how" as an early-section variant. Unified metaphor: transit / luggage / walking forward without a map.

---

**[Verse 1]**

Eleventh floor, the glass still trembling
Cement truck rolling through my ribs at eighty
Morning light, the room won't hold its breath
I zip the last bag shut, forget the rest
Corridor echoes, elevator slow
Wheels catch the tile as the doors let go
Behind me, walls that never learned my name
Ahead, the road I walked through shame and flame
I leave the key where the landlord said
And roll my luggage toward the morning thread

**[Pre-Chorus]**

The station lights cut through the morning grey
Shenzhen North opens like a cold bright mouth
A family of four, four screens, no one looks away
I drag my suitcase through that quiet race

**[Chorus]**

I don't even know
I don't even know
I don't even know where's next step
The platform hums, the timetable blinks
I can't go back through those same old links
I don't even know

**[Verse 2]**

Back home the stall still fries the same three starches
Fifteen years, same black pan, same arches
Father at the table, same soft lines again —
"Chew slow, hold the bowl" — ten years, the same refrain
Brother's phone lights up the dinner air
I sit between them like I'm barely there
I can visit home, but I can't stay long
The hallway knows my footsteps don't belong
Gravity pulls soft, then lets me free
I pack again before it swallows me

**[Bridge]**

Muddy lake road from the house by the water —
childhood clay still stuck under my collar
Was it ever the room, or just the weight of standing still?
I set the phone face-down and lift the bag

**[Final Chorus]**

The board above me flickers, all the names dissolve
But my feet remember — they don't need to solve

I don't even know where's next step
