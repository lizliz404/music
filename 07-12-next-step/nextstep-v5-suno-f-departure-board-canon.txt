```
Electroacoustic pulse minimalism, phase-shifting mallet and muted-synth loops, granular station clicks, warm restrained sub pulse, close-miked soft male sung recitation, three-note melodic cells, whispered unison echo layers, 92 BPM D minor
```

### Exclude Styles


### Sliders
Weirdness 62-74 · Style Influence 64-76

### Production Notes
- Interlude: The pulse cuts completely; breaths trigger tiny granular reflections while the lead stays near-ASMR and almost dry.
- Outro: Phase loops lose one note per cycle, the sub disappears, whispered doubles vanish, and a bare exhale remains against faint electrical hum.
