# Impotent Toward Reality

**Genre:** Alt-rock / post-punk with cinematic electronic undertow  
**Mood:** seductive → hollow → clear-eyed → quietly defiant  
**BPM feel:** mid-tempo (~96–104), room for a late surge

---

## Lyrics

[Intro]
Soft glow, soft glow —
another night on loan.
Feed me the highlight reel,
leave me alone with the aftertaste.

[Verse 1]
They packed a lifetime of thunder
into two hours flat.
Conflict, kiss, the big reveal —
I watched it all from where I sat.
Out there, love takes weather and time.
In here, I tap and it arrives.
Cheap, dense, on demand —
and somehow I call that alive.

[Pre-Chorus]
Then I turn my head to the ordinary day —
no score, no cut, no easy blaze.
Life was never thin.
I made fiction my measuring stick.

[Chorus]
I'm impotent toward reality —
threshold up, nothing hits like the screen.
Supernormal sugar in my veins,
normal concentration tastes too clean.
I'm impotent toward reality —
I know the meal, I still chew the lie.
Junk food heaven, empty calories —
I'm fed, but I don't feel alive.

[Verse 2]
High school phone light in the dark,
someone else's edited life.
Am I the battery in their game,
an NPC with nowhere to arrive?
Consumption feels like cash on hand —
payday dopamine, nothing left by dawn.
Production's a slow deposit —
scarce, compounding, hard-won.

[Pre-Chorus]
So ask it straight when the credits roll:
does this push me out, or bury me whole?
Imagination's a room I can rent —
not a house I pretend to own.

[Chorus]
I'm impotent toward reality —
threshold up, nothing hits like the screen.
Supernormal sugar in my veins,
normal concentration tastes too clean.
I'm impotent toward reality —
I know the meal, I still chew the lie.
Junk food heaven, empty calories —
I'm fed, but I don't feel alive.

[Bridge]
City walls, city walls —
inside can't leave, outside can't crawl in.
Red pill burning on my tongue,
Matrix soft as a second skin.
Brain in a vat, future so smooth —
staying under becomes the default move.
I don't need to spit every bite —
I need to know what I'm holding tonight.
Win the game by walking out —
not by sneering from the crowd.

[Final Chorus]
I'm impotent toward reality —
but I can lower what I've raised.
Crowd the bad out with something made —
leave a mark that isn't borrowed praise.
Not a saint, not a cynic either —
just a body choosing denser days.
Production over pure escape —
I want the hard joy that stays.

[Outro]
Soft glow, soft glow —
I put the phone face down.
Not cured. Not pure.
Just awake enough to leave the town.
