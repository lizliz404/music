## 《生在温差里 v3》
### (the curses, speaking)

## STYLE PROMPT (Suno)

Dark-warm Mandarin chamber alt-pop, 80 BPM, D minor verses (close, cold, intimate) with brief F-major warmth lifting the choruses without ever fully equalizing — two temperatures, unresolved. Vocal: a close-mic quartet of soft curses, patient and sly, almost consoling; they sing sweetly but one degree wrong — like something that should not know how to comfort you, comforting you. A single human lead gains clarity and weight only in the bridge and final chorus as the spirit stack thins. Texture: felt piano, double-bass harmonics, breath-heavy synth pad, a faint notification click as pulse; chorus opens as layered whispers becoming hushed harmony over cool autumn strings. The climax is not louder — it is the human voice finally being clearer than the curses. **Critical: every tender curse line must carry an audible wrongness. Do NOT render Verse 2 and Pre-Chorus 2 as sincere Mandopop balladry — sweetness with a crack in it.**
Exclude: anime-theme bombast, trap hats, metal guitar, jump-scare SFX, cartoon ghost voices, arena belting, key-change victory lap, gospel climax, pure sincere Mandopop, dual-lover karaoke duet.
