# Craft excerpt for this song (guidelines, not laws)

## Structure
- Common: V/C/V/C/B/C. Or invent.
- Section ownership: each section owns ONE thematic element.
- Bridge = one emotional core + one central question; changes the terms so final chorus means more.

## Lyrics that work
- Show with concrete images; plain speech when it hits harder.
- Hook: memorable, title-aligned, lands in chorus.
- Prosody: say aloud; stressed syllables matter.
- Mix perfect / slant rhyme; avoid nursery-rhyme lock OR total laziness.

## Emotional arc
- Contrast: sparse before dense; whisper before open; silence is an instrument.
- Final chorus should escalate meaning (not just volume).

## Suno-ready draft (include in output)
- Parameterized vocal tags on every section (structure + delivery).
- 2–3 distinct emotional containers: compact style field + single-line comma exclude each.
- Song profile: title, rough BPM/key if chosen, hook line, short arrangement note.
- Changelog line for this version.

## Anti-patterns for THIS material
- Do not write a theory essay set to music.
- Do not moralize CY as villain or saint.
- Do not default to pure dual-lover “you are summer I am autumn” cover-song energy unless hybrid still keeps spirit POV.
- Do not outline. Full real lines only.
