# BETWEEN TEMPERATURES
### (the soft curses, speaking) — EN v2 (audit-raised)

## NOTE
Style: hybrid kept — spirits speak; summer/autumn = weather between hot-word / cool-bandwidth humans, not dual-lover ballad.
v2 vs B v1: fix fragments into singable lines; center translation-error chorus (from A); keep “perfect theories” as post-chorus kill-shot; merge bridge into one arc; clear avoid-hand image; misaligned-sweet vocal tags; less script, more song.

## STYLE — draft direction
Dark-warm alt ballad / soft gothic chamber-electronic, ~78–82 BPM, D minor with brief major lifts that don’t fully resolve. Target ~3:15.
Vocal: close multi-voice spirits — on tender sections, sing sweetly but with wrongness: like something that shouldn’t know how to comfort is comforting. Never pure romantic sincerity.
Journey: phone glow → sparse verse → pre → chorus (translation error) → post (names + perfect theories) → v2 uncanny tender → pre2 other lights → chorus → one continuous bridge (test + action) → final chorus human clearer → whisper outro.

## FULL LYRICS

[Intro]
[Whispered] [Intimate] [Low Energy]
[close multi-voice, phone-glow room hum]

We don’t live in either of you.
We live in the weather between —
where summer floods a chat,
and autumn answers three words clean.

[Verse 1]
[Breathy] [Smooth] [Low Energy]

I am suspicion — I write subtitles under silence.
I am cold-heart — I turn June into early fall.
I am anxiety — I count to a hundred on the send key.
I am avoid — I grip your hand so it won’t reach out.

We are not you.
We are not the one on the other screen.
We only live where two weathers meet:
desk lamp on unfinished books,
and a chat box breathing for the room.

[Pre-Chorus]
[Smooth] [Building Energy] [Harmonies]

From the other side, soft:
“I read you.
I just don’t answer one by one —
only where I have something real.”

That’s a clean map of today’s bandwidth.
No door closing. No colder sun.
But we are experts at dubbing the blank.

[Chorus]
[Intimate → Open] [Medium Energy] [Harmonies]

We live between temperatures —
one side high summer, one side autumn rain.
We take “I’m out of strength today”
and flip it into “I don’t matter to you.”

We live between temperatures —
every blue tick a piece of proof.
When trust becomes a stack of receipts,
that’s when we get our names.

[Post-Chorus]
[Whispered] [Call-and-response] [Low Energy]
[dissonant close cluster]

Suspicion. Cold-heart.
Anxiety. Avoid.
Every time you speak our names,
we grow a body.

We prefer your perfect theories —
they feed us forever
and never ask us to die.

[Verse 2]
[Breathy] [Intimate] [Medium Energy]
[sweet but misaligned — a curse comforting you, uncanny, NOT pure romantic sincerity]

You say: this wave will pass.
The long roof won’t leak.
We love that gentle verdict —
every water stain can be called weather.

If every sting goes back to childhood,
if every crack gets covered with “we’re safe,”
we never have to prove we exist —
and we never have to leave.

[Pre-Chorus 2]
[Smooth] [Building Energy] [Harmonies]
[sweet misalignment, uncanny comfort]

When the whole city has one window left,
you pour all the night into that pane.
We grow fat on the only light —
we fear the other rooms turning on.

Those lamps aren’t a show for anyone.
If one goes dark, it doesn’t mean the sky dies.

[Chorus]
[Intimate → Open] [Medium Energy] [Harmonies]

We live between temperatures —
one side high summer, one side autumn rain.
We take “I’m out of strength today”
and flip it into “I don’t matter to you.”

We live between temperatures —
every blue tick a piece of proof.
When trust becomes a stack of receipts,
that’s when we get our names.

Two hearts don’t owe the same night
to earn the right to trust.

[Bridge]
[Breathy] [Intimate] [Low → Medium Energy]
[dry solo → human lead gains weight; curses recede to distant whispers]

Don’t ask if line twelve got an echo.
Ask only this:

When you really fall —
do those hands show up?

We hate that question.
Because it has an answer.
Once missing isn’t a verdict.
Missing for good — stop calling it chance.

Don’t just sketch the exit on paper.
Open the door.
Call the numbers you never dialed.
Sing the drafts you never voiced.

Let a second light, a third light, come on.
Don’t let one person hold the whole roof.
Every real road you actually walk
takes a face from us.

[Final Chorus]
[Open] [Medium Energy] [Harmonies] [Hushed, not loud]
[human lead clearer than dissolving spirit choir]

We live between temperatures —
one side high summer, one side autumn rain.
But “I’m out of strength today”
can also just be honesty.

We can starve between temperatures
when you stop rushing the translation.
Trust isn’t a line-by-line receipt —
and it isn’t infinite credit either.

If the imbalance stays and no one repairs it,
don’t hide the crack under “we’re fine.”
If you fall and those hands are still here —
let us scatter tonight.

[Outro]
[Whispered] [Fading]

You called us temporary —
then let tomorrow testify.

If when it matters, that person comes —
we crawl back into the rain.

If again and again, no one comes —
don’t call us overthinking.

[End]
