# BETWEEN TEMPERATURES
### (the soft curses, speaking) — hermes-agent EN v2

## NOTE
Style choice: hybrid (b). Soft 咒怨 keep full subjecthood — suspicion, cold-heart, anxiety, avoid — speaking as a patient quartet that feeds on gaps between two human bandwidths. Summer/autumn = weather they live in (hot flood of paragraphs vs clean honest short), NOT a dual-lover “I am summer / you are autumn” ballad.

What changed vs B v1 (surgical, from audit):
1) Centered B’s best line — “We prefer your perfect theories — they feed us forever and never ask us to die” — as post-chorus (replaces weak didactic ledger counting).
2) Fixed fragment-script feel (“Not a colder sun. Not a door.” → full singable picture lines).
3) Chorus rebuilt around A’s translation-error mechanism: tired honesty mistranslated as “you don’t matter” — first-listen hook with equal clarity.
4) No double-turn: one continuous bridge (falsifiable question + pattern test + other lights / real doors) — Turn arc folded in, not a second climax.
5) Avoid-hand clarified: Avoid grips *your* hand so *it* won’t reach out.
6) Vocal tags: misaligned sweetness throughout — tender like a curse comforting you; not pure romantic Mandopop sincerity.
7) Hybrid spirit POV retained; humans are weather sources and one testable presence, not co-singers in a lovers’ duet.

---

## STYLE (Suno — misalignment critical)

Dark-warm Mandopop-adjacent alt ballad with soft gothic chamber-electronic underglow, ~78–80 BPM, D minor with brief F-major warmth that never fully equalizes (two temperatures, unresolved). Target ~3:15–3:25. Vocal: close-mic multi-voice spirit stack — patient, sly, almost consoling; sing sweetly but with a one-degree-wrong comfort, like something that should not know how to soothe you, soothing you. Not diary confessional, not cartoon horror, not dual-lover duet sincerity. Journey: phone-screen glow + room hum → sparse verse (felt piano, soft pulse, faint message-chime) → pre thickens with low choir-air and warm pad under glass → chorus opens as layered whispers becoming hushed harmony over cool autumn strings → v2 desk-lamp / page-turn texture → bridge strips to one dry voice + bare piano → final chorus wider but still hushed, human clarity rising as spirit stack thins, dual-temperature pad left unresolved. Exclude: trap drop, metal scream, arena belt, glossy max-K-pop, parody horror FX, cartoon ghost comedy, pure dual-lover summer/autumn karaoke energy, gospel climax, key-change victory lap, pure romantic sincerity without misalignment.

## FULL LYRICS

[Intro]
[Whispered] [Intimate] [Low Energy]
[close multi-voice, phone-glow room hum]
[sweet but slightly wrong — four soft curses breathing]

Twelve lines of blue light.
One short voice note landing soft.
You haven’t named it cold yet —
we already have a pulse.

[Verse 1]
[Breathy] [Smooth] [Low Energy]
[four distinct spirits, restrained; tender like a curse comforting you]

I am suspicion — I write the silence a side note.
I am cold-heart — I blow June into early fall.
I am anxiety — I count to a hundred on the send key.
I am avoid — I grip your hand so it won’t reach out.

We are not you.
We are not the person on the other screen.
We only live where two weathers meet —
one desk lamp crushing unfinished books,
one chat box breathing for the whole room.

[Pre-Chorus 1]
[Smooth] [Building Energy] [Harmonies]
[single warm lead; whispers answer last words — misaligned sweetness]

She said, soft from the other side:
“I read you. I just don’t answer line by line.
Where I have a thought, I answer.”

That was only today’s bandwidth, said clean —
no colder sun, no slammed door.
But we are experts at dubbing the blank.

[Chorus 1]
[Intimate → Open] [Medium Energy] [Harmonies]
[open lead + soft spectral choir; sweet, unsettled, not pure love-song]

We live between temperatures —
one side high summer, one side autumn rain.
We take “I really have no strength today”
and turn it into “you already don’t matter.”

We live between temperatures —
we make a witness of every “read.”
When trust becomes a stack of receipts,
that’s when we get our names.

[Post-Chorus]
[Whispered] [Call-and-Response] [Low Energy]
[four names in close cluster; then the theory bite — still tender, still wrong]

Suspicion… cold-heart…
Anxiety… avoid…
Every time you speak a name,
we grow a body.

We prefer your perfect theories —
they feed us forever
and never ask us to die.

[Verse 2]
[Breathy] [Smooth] [Medium Energy]
[intimate lead, patient, almost consoling — curse-comfort, not romance]

You said: this weather passes soon.
Long roofs don’t leak for long.
We love that gentle final word —
every water mark can be called “just weather.”

If every sting gets billed to old wounds,
if every crack gets sealed with “secure base,”
we never have to prove we exist —
and we never have to truly leave.

[Pre-Chorus 2]
[Smooth] [Building Energy] [Harmonies]
[breathy lead into two-part; warm sixths with whispered undertone — misaligned sweet]

When the whole city is one window,
you pour every night into that frame.
We grow fat on that single lamp —
what we fear is other rooms going bright.

Other lights aren’t for show.
They just mean one blackout is not the whole sky.

[Chorus 2]
[Intimate → Open] [Medium Energy] [Harmonies]
[fuller curse choir, still hushed; sweetness with wrong grain]

We live between temperatures —
one side high summer, one side autumn rain.
We take “I really have no strength today”
and turn it into “you already don’t matter.”

We live between temperatures —
we make a witness of every “read.”
When trust becomes a stack of receipts,
that’s when we get our names.

Two hearts don’t owe the same long nights
to earn the right to trust.

[Bridge]
[Breathy] [Intimate] [Low Energy → Building]
[sparse piano / one dry voice first; spirits thin; continuous arc — no second turn section]
[still almost gentle — the question is the knife]

Don’t ask if line twelve got an echo.
Ask the only question that starves us:

When you truly start to fall —
do those hands come, or not?

We hate that question.
It has weather with an answer.
Once is not a verdict.
If they never come, stop calling it chance.

Don’t only draw exits on paper —
open a real door.
Call the number you keep saving.
Sing the draft you keep analyzing.

Let a second light, a third light, come on.
Let your world not hang on one person alone.
Every real road you take
costs us another face.

[Final Chorus]
[Open] [Medium Energy] [Harmonies] [Hushed, not loud]
[clearer human lead over dissolving spirit choir; warm major lift, minor bass unresolved]
[sweetness thinning — less curse-comfort, more clean air]

We live between temperatures —
one side high summer, one side autumn rain.
But “I really have no strength today”
can also just mean honest.

We can starve between temperatures
when you stop rushing to translate.
Trust is not a line-by-line receipt —
and not an open-ended overdraft.

If the imbalance stays and no one repairs it,
don’t wallpaper the crack with “secure.”
If when you fall, those hands are still here —
tonight, let us scatter.

[Outro]
[Whispered] [Fading]
[one centered whisper; ghost choir disappears line by line]

You call us temporary —
then let tomorrow testify.

If when it matters, that person comes,
we fold back into the rain.

If again and again, no one comes —
don’t call us overthinking.

[End]
