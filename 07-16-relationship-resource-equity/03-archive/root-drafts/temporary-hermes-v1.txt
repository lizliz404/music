# BETWEEN TEMPERATURES
### (the soft curses, speaking) — hermes-agent first draft

## NOTE
Style choice: hybrid (b). Spirits keep full subjecthood (JJK 咒怨 / Exactly On Time device); summer/autumn is weather they feed between — one human runs hot with words, one runs cool with honest bandwidth — NOT a dual-lover summer/autumn ballad. Stole from 范玮琪 only: dual-temperature imagery, gentle long-vowel singability, complementary contrast without smearing spirit agency into “you and I are seasons.” Emphasized: temporary = cover story that never ends; trust ≠ ledger; “read but don’t reply one by one” as clean report not cold door; optionality as action not analysis. Left out: CY as villain/saint, equity-theory lecture, DPDR monologue, unfalsifiable lullaby without a bite.

---

## STYLE (draft direction)

Dark-warm Mandopop-adjacent alt ballad with soft gothic chamber-electronic underglow, ~76–82 BPM, D minor with brief major lifts that don’t fully resolve (two temperatures that never equalize). Target ~3:20. Vocal: close-mic, slightly otherworldly multi-voice — spirits answering each other, patient and sly, almost tender toward the humans they haunt; not diary confessional, not cartoon horror, not dual-lover duet. Journey: phone-screen glow + room hum → sparse verse (felt piano, soft pulse, faint message-chime) → pre thickens with low choir-air and warm pad (summer heat under glass) → chorus opens as layered whispers becoming harmony over cool autumn strings → v2 adds desk-lamp / page-turn texture → bridge strips to one dry voice + bare piano → final chorus wider but still hushed, harmonic not loud, dual-temperature pad left unresolved. Exclude: trap drop, metal scream, arena belt, glossy max-K-pop, parody horror FX, cartoon ghost comedy, pure dual-lover summer/autumn karaoke energy, gospel climax, key-change victory lap.

## LYRICS

[Intro]
[Whispered] [Intimate] [Low Energy]
[close multi-voice, phone-glow room hum]

We don't live in either of you.
We live in the weather between —
where summer floods a chat,
and autumn answers three words clean.

[Verse 1]
[Breathy] [Smooth] [Low Energy]

I am suspicion — I count the blank after blue.
I am cold-heart — I cool the room when the long note lands soft.
I am anxiety — I turn a kindness into a score.
I am avoid — I lift your thumb off send before it goes.

We are not her exam stack.
We are not your midnight flood.
We are soft curses with names you like —
guess, freeze, spin, run.

[Pre-Chorus]
[Smooth] [Building Energy] [Harmonies]

Don't hand us the childhood.
Don't hand us the desk lamp and the books.
We only need a gap to drink from —
a gap is enough.

[Chorus]
[Intimate → Open] [Medium Energy] [Harmonies]

We live between temperatures —
your summer of paragraphs,
her autumn of honest short.
Call us temporary rain,
call us short-term noise —
we still grow in the weather report.

We live between temperatures —
that's why you never catch us whole.
If every ache gets wrapped in “long cycle,”
we never have to go.

[Verse 2]
[Breathy] [Smooth] [Medium Energy]

She said: I read you.
I just don't answer one by one.
Not a colder sun. Not a door.
A clean bandwidth map — the day already full.

But we rewrote it for you
into less heat, less proof, less love.
We made a ledger out of silence
and sold you the math.

You almost starved us —
traded “she's careless” for “I'm scarce.”
Good. That thins our smoke.
We still sit between two phones
and wait for the next soft gap.

[Pre-Chorus]
[Smooth] [Building Energy] [Harmonies]

Devotion tastes like one plug in the wall.
Other rooms stay dark if you only plan them.
We live on beautiful unspent light —
analysis is a feast; action is a drought.

[Chorus]
[Intimate → Open] [Medium Energy] [Harmonies]

We live between temperatures —
your summer of paragraphs,
her autumn of honest short.
Call us temporary rain,
call us short-term noise —
we still grow in the weather report.

We live between temperatures —
that's why you never catch us whole.
If every ache gets wrapped in “long cycle,”
we never have to go.

[Bridge]
[Breathy] [Intimate] [Low Energy]
[sparse piano / one dry voice]

Trust is not a scoreboard.
Two people can love uneven hours
and still not be in debt.

The question that starves us is quiet:
when it matters — is she there?
Not: did she answer line four of twelve.

We hate that question.
It has weather with an answer.
We prefer your perfect theories —
they feed us forever
and never ask us to die.

[Final Chorus]
[Open] [Medium Energy] [Harmonies] [Hushed, not loud]

We live between temperatures —
unless you stop making us the whole sky.
Turn on lights that aren't only hers.
Keep a life that holds if the chat goes dim.

We live between temperatures —
say “temporary” like a fact, not a song to soothe.
Then do the boring proof:
show up elsewhere.
Leave less of us
in the weather between.

[Outro]
[Whispered] [Fading]

Summer flood…
autumn short…
long cycle…
temporary—

(if you let the gap stay ours.)

[End]
