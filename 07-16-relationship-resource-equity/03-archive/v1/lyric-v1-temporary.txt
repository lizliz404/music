# TEMPORARY
### (the curses, speaking) — v1
# Session axis (Liz): 负面情绪 = 咒怨 / 有主体与 consciousness · 参照 Exactly On Time 的 idea-as-speaker · 视角清奇但内容不必复杂

## CONCEPT (not sung)
猜忌 / 寒心 / 焦虑 / 回避 as soft cursed spirits — born in the gap between a long message and a short reply.
They keep the ledger. They call themselves temporary. They have no falsification condition.
Secure trust is the light that starves them; bandwidth report is not food, but they try to eat it anyway.

## STYLE (draft direction)
Dark-intimate alt-pop / soft gothic chamber-electronic, ~78–84 BPM, minor key with warm major lifts that never fully resolve. Target ~3:20.
Vocal: close-mic, slightly otherworldly, multi-voice stack on chorus like small spirits answering each other — not diary confessional, not cartoon horror. Patient, sly, almost protective.
Journey: phone-screen glow + room hum → sparse verse (one synth figure, soft pulse) → pre thickens with low choir-air → chorus opens as layered whispers becoming harmony → v2 adds exam-room / desk noise texture → bridge strips to one dry voice + bare piano → final chorus wider but still hushed, not loud.
Exclude: trap drop, metal scream, arena belt, glossy K-pop max production, parody horror FX, cartoon ghost comedy.

## LYRICS

[Intro]
[Whispered] [Intimate] [Low Energy]
[close multi-voice, almost in your ear]

We live in the three dots.
We live in the blue tick.
We live where your long paragraph
meets her three-word reply.

[Verse 1]
[Breathy] [Smooth] [Low Energy]

I am suspicion — I count the seconds she doesn't type.
I am cold-heart — I cool the room when the voice note ends soft.
I am anxiety — I make a ledger out of kindness.
I am avoid — I pull your hand back from the send.

We are not her.
We are not you.
We are the weather that rolls through
when the signal is fine
and the meaning is not.

[Pre-Chorus]
[Smooth] [Building Energy] [Harmonies]

Don't blame the childhood.
Don't blame the exam books stacked to the lamp.
Blame is a meal — we only need a gap.
A gap is enough.

[Chorus]
[Intimate → Open] [Medium Energy] [Harmonies]

We are temporary —
that's what you named us so we could stay.
Cover us with the long cycle,
call us noise, call us short-term rain —
we still drink from the delay.

We are temporary —
no test can kill a ghost that writes the test.
If every ache gets explained away,
we never have to leave.

[Verse 2]
[Breathy] [Smooth] [Medium Energy]

She said: I read you.
I just don't answer one by one.
Honest bandwidth — a clean report.
Not a door closing. Not a colder sun.

But we translated it for you
into a debit on the trust.
We turned a fact into a scene:
"See? Less heat. Less proof."

You almost caught us —
swapped "she's careless" for "I'm scarce."
Good. That starves a little.
But we still sit on the chair
between two phones.

[Pre-Chorus]
[Smooth] [Building Energy] [Harmonies]

Devotion tastes like one plug in the wall.
Optionality is light in other rooms —
analysis alone won't flip those switches.
We know. We live on unspent plans.

[Chorus]
[Intimate → Open] [Medium Energy] [Harmonies]

We are temporary —
that's what you named us so we could stay.
Cover us with the long cycle,
call us noise, call us short-term rain —
we still drink from the delay.

We are temporary —
no test can kill a ghost that writes the test.
If every ache gets explained away,
we never have to leave.

[Bridge]
[Breathy] [Intimate] [Low Energy — sparse piano / dry voice]

Trust is not a scoreboard.
Two people can love uneven hours
and still not be in debt.

The real check is quieter:
when it matters — is she there?
Not: did she reply to line four of twelve.

We hate that question.
It has an answer.
We prefer your beautiful theories —
they feed us forever
and never ask us to die.

[Final Chorus]
[Open] [Medium Energy] [Harmonies] [Hushed, not loud]

We are temporary —
unless you keep us as the whole weather.
Starve us with other lights on,
with hands that aren't only hers,
with a life that holds even if the chat goes dim.

We are temporary —
say it like a fact, not a lullaby.
Then go do the boring proof:
show up elsewhere.
Leave less of us
between replies.

[Outro]
[Whispered] [Fading]

Three dots…
blue tick…
long cycle…
temporary—

(if you let us be.)
