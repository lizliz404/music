# SECOND CIRCLE
## Inspired by "宇宙市长" (Universe Mayor) — a dormant idea, rediscovered
## 2026-07-11

================================================================================
                        STYLE / GENRE PROMPT
                      (Suno Style field)
================================================================================

Reflective alt-pop with indie electronic texture, 95 BPM, warm mid-tempo,
E major with occasional minor borrowings for tension. Female vocalist: intimate,
breathy, slightly hushed — like someone reading an old diary aloud to themselves
in an empty room. Never theatrical, never belted. Journey: soft piano and tape
hiss open the song like a memory surfacing. Verse 1 stays sparse — minimal beat,
dusty vinyl warmth, a single repeating piano figure. Pre-chorus lifts slightly
with a swelling pad and a second vocal harmony. Chorus opens wider — gentle
four-on-the-floor kick, warm bass, layered backing vocals like echoes of a
younger self. Verse 2 adds a subtle arpeggiated synth, pulse-like, the sound of
thoughts connecting. Bridge strips to just voice and piano — the most intimate
moment — then final chorus returns fuller, more assured, like an idea that's
finally found its shape. Outro fades to tape hiss and the soft click of a
notebook closing. Production: warm, analog-leaning, intentional space between
notes — nostalgia without sentimentality, wonder without naivety.

Exclude styles: dramatic orchestral, trap, metal, belted pop, EDM drop,
overproduced, glossy.

and waits for you to catch up

the last one standing wins
