# "Next Step" v5 — Style I: California Hills
## Eagles × 李宗盛 融合方向
## BPM/Key: 92 BPM, D minor

### 融合逻辑
取《Hotel California》的加州暖调摇滚骨架——双吉他交织对话、模拟录音的宽阔声场、不急于进副歌的叙事型结构；取《山丘》念白式的低沉男声、留白大于填充的吐字节奏、将情感重量交给语气而非音量的克制。不愤怒，不感伤，只是在走。

---

### Style Prompt
```
70s California rock, conversational folk-rock, dual clean electric guitars weaving harmonies, warm analog live-room production, baritone talk-singing narrative vocal, unhurried cinematic phrasing, Hammond organ subtle pads, brushed drums
```

### Exclude Styles
```
pop, pop-punk, emo, radio polish, autotune, trap, EDM, synth-pop, dream-pop, belting, shouting, screaming, lo-fi, garage rock, excessive distortion, guitar shredding, arena rock, hard rock, country rock, digital production, four-on-the-floor, electronic drums
```

### Sliders
Weirdness: 30 · Style Influence: 75

---

### 制作要点

**Interlude 处理**：只剩一把 clean guitar arpeggio + 人声。murmured/loose 的念白推到最亲密——听者应该能听到句与句之间的呼吸。Dual guitars 仅以 volume-pedal swell 和 barely-there harmonics 出现。

**Outro 处理**：双吉他逐把褪去。到 "One step more" 只剩一把吉他——clean、不赶、不加 reverb wash。最后的 "and that's enough" 落在单把人声，或人声 + 一个 sustained guitar harmonic decay——不是 fade-out，是 deliberate ending。

**Instrumental Break（新结构）**：Bridge 之后、Interlude 之前，8-16 bars。双 clean 电吉他 call and response——不是炫技，是对话。melodic not technical。回应歌的中心问题：往前走，还是只是不回去？让随后的 Interlude 成为这个对话之后的 exhale。

---

### 分段人声标签
```
[Verse 1: conversational low baritone, clean guitar arpeggio, unhurried — voice and guitar only]
[Pre-Chorus: vocal intensity rises slightly, band enters subtly, dual guitars warm and wide]
[Chorus: full arrangement, controlled baritone — no belting, dual guitars soar but vocal stays grounded]
[Verse 2: conversational delivery returns, acoustic backbone, organ pad underneath]
[Bridge: stripped back, near-spoken, single guitar, vulnerability — the question hangs]
[Instrumental Break: dual clean electric guitars, call and response, melodic not technical]
[Interlude: murmured, breath-close, sparse guitar harmonics, near-ASMR intimacy]
[Final Chorus: dual guitars wide and cinematic, vocal remains controlled — conviction through composure not volume]
[Outro: slowing, almost spoken, guitars fade one by one, voice alone at the last word]
```
