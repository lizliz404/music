Key principles to apply:

- Rhyme types: perfect, family, assonance, consonance, slant — mix them

- Meter: stressed syllables matter more than count; say it out loud

- Show, don't tell where possible
- The hook should be the line people remember

## Suno AI Prompt Engineering Guidelines

When building the production prompt, follow these rules:

### Style/Genre Description Formula
Genre + Mood + Era + Instruments + Vocal Style + Production + Dynamics

Describe the JOURNEY, not just the genre:
"Begins as a haunting whisper over sparse piano. Gradually layers in muted brass. Builds through the chorus with full orchestra..."

### Metatags to Use (place in [brackets])
Structure: [Intro] [Verse] [Pre-Chorus] [Chorus] [Bridge] [Outro] [End]
Vocal: [Whispered] [Spoken Word] [Belted] [Falsetto] [Powerful] [Harmonies]
Dynamics: [High Energy] [Low Energy] [Building Energy] [Explosive] [Emotional Climax]
Atmosphere: [Melancholic] [Euphoric] [Nostalgic] [Dreamy] [Intimate]
Gender: [Female Vocals] [Male Vocals]

### Rules
- NO artist names or trademarks — describe the sound
- Specify BPM and key when relevant
- Build a vocal PERSONA, not just a gender
- Unexpected genre combos can be gold
- Custom Mode: separate Style + Lyrics fields
- Lyrics field limit: ~3,000 chars (~40-60 lines)
- 5-8 metatags per section max
