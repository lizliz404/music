# NO THIRD DOOR (Cash Flow Body)
## Final — ready for Suno Custom Mode
## 2026-07-11

================================================================================
                        STYLE / GENRE PROMPT
                      (Suno Style field)
================================================================================

Upbeat electronic hip-hop with a melodic pop-rap chorus, 110 BPM, bright G major.
Male vocalist: confident rapper-singer with a playful, self-deprecating grin —
urban edge, slight rasp, never aggressive, never brooding. Journey: intimate
spoken-word intro over sparse kick, warm filtered synth pulse, distant footstep
foley. Verse 1 snaps in — crisp 808s, bouncy synth stabs like climbing footsteps,
locked-in rap groove. Pre-chorus lifts with stacked harmonies and building pads.
Chorus explodes — wide supersaw chords, punchy four-on-the-floor drums, belted
anthemic hook with gang-vocal chant on the tag line. Verse 2 tighter rap pocket,
denser internal rhymes, more syncopation. Bridge strips to half-time — airy
pads, breathy near-falsetto, sparse percussion, contemplative. Final chorus
detonates — full production, choir-layered harmonies, escalated variation on the
hook (not a repeat). Outro fades to spoken word over synth drone and footsteps,
warm smiling close. Production: clean modern electronic, analog warmth on the
bass, rhythmic step-motif woven through the low end — sovereign, kinetic,
city-at-sunset energy, zero melancholy.

Exclude styles: dark, sad, lo-fi chill, trap mumble, metal, acoustic folk,
minor-key ballad, dramatic orchestral.
