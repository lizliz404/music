# NO THIRD DOOR (Cash Flow Body)

A song about stair-climbing as philosophy, sovereignty, and parallel cognition — adapted from Liz's Chinese prose into English hip-hop/electronic.

## Source Material

Seven files of Chinese articles about 爬楼 (stair climbing), written by Liz from April 2024 to December 2025. The original material covers:
- Physical benefits (free, weather-proof, HIIT + cardio hybrid)
- Parallel cognition (voice-to-text writing while climbing)
- Three-stage evolution (adaptation → multitasking → flow state dependency)
- Fire escape as HSP sanctuary
- Emotional cooling and bird's-eye perspective
- Commercial paradox (nobody monetizes stairs)
- Building anthropology (observing society through stairwells)

## Final Version

**v2 NO THIRD DOOR (Cash Flow Body)** — integrated by Claude Sonnet 5.0, curated by Liz.

Takes:
- A's precision ("cash flow / vault" — the stock vs flow distinction)
- B's chant hook ("sweat or suffer, no third door" — gang vocal space)
- Rewritten final chorus with actual variation (not just a louder repeat)
- New callback line: "keep the current flowing" (triple meaning: cash flow current, water current, current account)

→ [versions/v2-no-third-door-integrated.md](versions/v2-no-third-door-integrated.md)

## Key Decisions

1. **6000 vs 6700 words** — kept "six thousand" (2 syllables) over "six thousand seven hundred" (5 syllables) for rap flow, despite source accuracy favoring 6700
2. **"Legs are the root"** — kept clean metaphor over clinical terms (baseline, chassis). Understatement is the flex.
3. **capex/opex** — rejected for chorus. "Cash flow / vault" already more precise than 99% of pop lyrics; jargon would kill chant clarity.
