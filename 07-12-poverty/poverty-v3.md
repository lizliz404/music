## Production Notes

- **Album-architecture**: This is stage one — the "what is happening to me" song. It does not resolve. The cage door is visible but not yet walked through. Later songs in the cycle do the escape.
- **Bridge**: The hallway memory is the emotional center. Everything before it builds toward it; everything after it is lived in its shadow. Performed near-whisper with guitar alone — no band, no safety net.
- **Ending**: The shelf outlasts everything. The narrator is still in the room with it. Not triumphant. Not hopeless. Counting.
- **Suno generation**: Cherry-pick the take where the bridge drops to voice-and-guitar and the band's return on the extended chorus feels like a held breath finally released — not an explosion, a decision.
