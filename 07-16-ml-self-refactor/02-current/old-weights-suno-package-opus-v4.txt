# Old Weights — Suno Complete Package

## 1. Song profile

- **Title:** Old Weights
- **BPM range:** 76–82 (target 80; rubato-tolerant verses, don't quantize the phrasing)
- **Key lean:** A minor or D dorian — modal color, avoid bright major-key resolutions
- **Length target:** 3:00–3:20
- **Hook line:** `OLD WEIGHTS — when I'm soft, they fire.` (cold-open plants it; each chorus lands it)
- **Arrangement arc:** bare cold-open line → sparse intro → controlled verse → half-lift pre-chorus → dynamic-but-not-anthemic chorus → deeper verse → thinner pre → chorus → near-silent bridge (spoken-sung) → final chorus (denser, layered, **not louder**) → whispered outro decay
- **Performance discipline (this song only):** peak stays at mf+, never full belt — this is diagnosis, not lament. Close-mic, dry, audible breath, conversational-melodic. No falsetto, no melisma, no ad-libs after chorus. Leave one full beat of silence after every `OLD WEIGHTS —` (do not fill with instrument). Bridge is a live-take feel — a small vocal hesitation or crack is welcome. Final chorus grows by layering (one low octave double, texture), not by volume.
