# Task: Write a song based on the stair-climbing materials

## Context

You are writing song lyrics inspired by a series of Chinese articles about "爬楼" (stair climbing). The author (Liz) has been stair-climbing since April 2024 and has developed a rich philosophy around it. The materials include raw self-talk logs, AI-assisted outlines, and a polished three-part final draft.

## Core Concepts to Encode in the Song

1. **Body as cash flow, not savings** — health requires daily maintenance, you can't "save up" health
2. **Free / zero equipment / weather-proof** — stair climbing costs nothing, works in any weather
3. **Parallel processing** — body on auto-pilot while mind outputs (voice-to-text writing while climbing)
4. **Three-stage evolution**: physical adaptation → multitasking → flow state dependency (you NEED the moving state to think)
5. **Fire escape as HSP sanctuary** — zero-stimulus environment, no ads, no noise, perfect for highly sensitive people
6. **Bird's eye view** — perspective shift at the top, petty worries become insignificant
7. **Commercial paradox** — nobody promotes stair climbing because there's no money in it (Nike can't monetize stairs)
8. **Solitude ≠ Isolation** — it's nourishing alone time, not loneliness
9. **Emotional cooling** — climbing to process anger/anxiety, each floor a "cooling valve"
10. **"Either sweat or suffer — no third door"**
11. **6700 words produced in 60 floors** — the productivity miracle
12. **Legs are the foundation** — if legs fail, everything fails

## Reference: Songwriting Craft Guidelines

### Song Structure Options
- ABABCB (Verse/Chorus/Verse/Chorus/Bridge/Chorus) — most pop/rock
- AABA (Verse/Verse/Bridge/Verse) — jazz standards, ballads
- ABAB (Verse/Chorus alternating) — simple, direct
- AAA (Verse/Verse/Verse) — folk, storytelling

### Lyric Principles
- Show, don't tell where possible
- The hook should be the line people remember
- Mix perfect and slant rhymes to avoid nursery-rhyme feel
- Meter matters — say it out loud, if you stumble, revise
- Prosody: stable feelings pair with settled melodies; unstable feelings pair with wandering melodies
- Internal rhyme adds texture
- Silence and contrast are instruments

### For Rap Sections
- Dense syllable packing, internal rhymes, unexpected word pairs
- Confident delivery, not aggressive
- Can be playful and witty
