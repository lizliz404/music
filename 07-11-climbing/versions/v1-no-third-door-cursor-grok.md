### Suno Style Prompt

Upbeat electronic hip-hop with melodic pop-rap chorus, 112 BPM, bright major
key (A or G). Confident male vocalist: swaggering half-MC half-singer, playful
grit, self-deprecating grin in the delivery — never dark or brooding. Journey:
intimate spoken intro over sparse kick and filtered synth pulse; Verse 1 snaps
in with tight 808s, crisp hi-hats, and bouncing synth stabs like climbing
footsteps; pre-chorus lifts with stacked harmonies; chorus explodes euphoric —
wide supersaw synths, punchy drums, belted hook with gang vocals chanting "no
third door." Verse 2 denser rap pocket, more syncopation. Bridge strips to airy
pads and breathy falsetto rooftop lift, then final chorus detonates with choir
layers and maximal energy. Outro spoken, warm, smiling fade. Production: clean
modern electronic, slight analog warmth, rhythmic step-motif in the bass, zero
melancholy — happy, sovereign, kinetic.

Exclude Styles: dark, sad, lo-fi chill, trap mumble, metal, acoustic folk,
minor-key ballad
