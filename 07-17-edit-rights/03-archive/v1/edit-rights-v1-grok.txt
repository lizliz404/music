SUNO STYLE PROMPT (Grok 4.5)

Style:
Dark indie-electronic art-pop, 98 BPM, A minor. Female vocal persona: dry-eyed observer who catalogs details like evidence, then cracks into clinical hunger — not theatrical rage. Verses: tight 808, clipped muted guitar, sterile pads, half-whispered close-mic, almost spoken, zero vibrato. Chorus: bass thickens, gated clap, stacked doubles on "Edit rights," melody lifts but stays locked — claim, not plea. Bridge: bare vocal + single piano + distant night-market hum. Final chorus: full surge, distorted low-end, shout-harmony on last "Why not me?," then hard cut — no fade, door slam. Dry, punchy, mid-forward; arc: whisper → claim → bare → roar → cut.

Exclude:
Warm acoustic folk, bright major-key pop, trap hi-hat spam, EDM drops, gospel runs, overly sweet R&B belting, lo-fi chillhop, cinematic strings swells, male vocal, fade-out endings, stadium rock bombast, chipmunk pitched vocals
