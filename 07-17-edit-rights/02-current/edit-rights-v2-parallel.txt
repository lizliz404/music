They stayed upstairs. She rents the empty time.
Counts cash like weather — soft laugh, small garden dream —
two keys, one building, money moving like steam.

I tutored a kid with a Toronto ticket in his name.

Whole wall of luxury, gold bars quiet in a drawer —
KFC coupons stuck to the cabinet like a dare.

[Pre-Chorus]
[breathy] [tighten] [flood rising]
Their "I deserve this" doesn't argue, doesn't perform.
Abundance walks barefoot. Scarcity keeps the score.

[Chorus]

[spoken-sung] [door-slam silence] [cold resolve]
Soft-close.
Fake vine.
Borrowed quiet —
mine to earn.

Dark mid-tempo alt-pop with a late-night hip-hop pocket, 94 BPM, F minor. Style:
Alt-pop with lo-fi electronic textures, 95 BPM, E minor. Female vocal — tired eyes, sharp tongue — close-mic and conversational in verses like 3AM confessions over bad coffee. Verse 1: sparse muted guitar, dry room ambience, a body alone with its exhaustion. Verse 2: ambient pad enters, sub-bass pulse — the wonder of stepping into frictionless space. Chorus: warm analog synth, gated clap on the backbeat, stacked vocal doubles on "edit rights" — lifts into a clear declarative melody, claiming not belting. Final section: distorted low-end creeps in, layered harmonies build, then hard cut to silence — door closes, no fade. Finger snaps on 2&4 in bridge. Production: dry verses, widening stereo reverb in chorus. Arc: exhaustion → wonder → claim → analysis → vulnerability → ignition → cut.

V2 对比笔记
═══════════════════════════════════════

开场策略:
  A: 直接切入 compound — "Ninety yuan. One night where the doors forget how to slam."
  B: 从 scarcity 世界铺陈 — 300 夜睡眠日志、垃圾车、借来的床，先建立「before」再切入 compound

Hook:
  A: "Edit rights — mute the world, keep the clean" (概念驱动，短促命令式，4 词短语)
  B: "Ninety yuan, ninety yuan / One night where the noise was gone / Edit rights, edit rights" (重复驱动，旋律型，90元做韵脚锚)

架构:
  A: Intro → V1 → Pre → C → V2 → Pre → C → Bridge → Breakdown → Final C → Outro (11 段)
  B: V1 → V2 → C → V3 → C → Bridge → Final (7 段)

Bridge 机制:
  A: 睡眠日志 counter-angle — "thin mattress, hallway noise" → "what's stopping mine?" (正面碰撞 scarcity vs abundance)
  B: 假花藤 + 夜市过渡 — "Fake vine on the basement pipe" → "Why not me? Why not me too?" (意象回响，轻声反问)

结尾色调:
  A: "Borrowed quiet — mine to earn" (冷收，宣示所有权，door-slam silence)
  B: "wanting is the first thing / Money can't delete" (哲思收，安抚型，wanting itself as freedom)

Vocal persona:
  A: dry close-mic observer → flood → quiet grin of hunger
  B: tired eyes sharp tongue, 3AM confessional → wonder → claim → ignition

BPM/Key:
  A: 94 F minor
  B: 95 E minor

最佳单行 (cross-version):
  A: "Every errand folded under the street like a secret name"
  B: "You can't edit the world from a hole"

源材料利用:
  A: sleep experiment 仅在 Bridge 出现（counterpoint）；auntie/Toronto 在 V2
  B: sleep experiment 占整个 V1（主视角起点）；auntie/Toronto 在 V3

共通亮点:
  - 双版皆 female vocal（v1 是一男一女）
  - 双版皆引入 sleep experiment 素材（v1 完全缺失）
  - 双版皆做了 hook singability 优化
  - 双版皆 new architecture（非 ABABCB）

Suno 适配:

  B: 更长（~55 行），emotion arc 更完整，V1 scarcity 铺陈可能需要更多 generation 磨合
