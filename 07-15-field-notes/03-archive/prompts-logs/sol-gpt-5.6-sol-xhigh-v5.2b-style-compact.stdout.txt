## 0. Diagnosis (short, Chinese OK)

这次 5+ 分钟不能简单归因于“短歌词仍然太长”。更可能的问题是：Style field 同时塞入了风格段、Lead/Female 说明和完整 Breath 编排，核心指令被重复、稀释，甚至互相拉扯。

- `compact / 3:00–3:30`要求短；
- `conversational long-line phrasing / rests / air / late strings / unresolved tension`倾向慢、宽、留白；
- 多个精确 bar 指令可能被当成必须逐项实现的段落事件；
- Lyrics 内又重复 two-bar、half-bar、one-bar 等要求，模型可能继续扩张过门；
- “3:00–3:30”不是确定性的时长参数，无法压过 narrative folk 的慢速结构先验；
- 不确定 Suno 是否截断长字段；即使没有截断，最重要的 genre、vocal、energy、length 信号也被制作手册淹没了。

所以这次不再删歌词主体。修复是：Style 只留约 400 字符的一个段落；Lyrics tag 只标段落功能；所有精确 bar 数删除。

## 1. Style A — FINAL compact (Suno-ready)

只把下面这一段粘进 **Style of Music**，不要把 Profile、Vocal Notes 或 Breath Notes 一起粘进去：

```text
intimate documentary folk, compact 84 BPM, D or E minor, immediate male vocal cold open, close-mic controlled melodic male, conversational verses, grounded descending chorus, never belted, fingerpicked acoustic guitar, warm upright bass, brushed dry drums, sparse room ambience, brief distant female wordless hum only in outro, restrained strings only in final chorus, tight 3-minute structure, no extra repeats or extended instrumentals
```

**Exclude Styles：**

```text
belting, shouting, soaring chorus, power ballad, arena rock, cinematic build, wall-to-wall vocals, choir pads, continuous female backing vocal, spoken-word only, long intro, long outro, repeated chorus, instrumental solo, extended interlude, slow ambient drift, heavy autotune
```

决定：

- 女声 hum 不删除，但只留在 Outro。Intro 的第一任务是让男声立即抓人；intro hum 可能诱发 pad、延迟主唱或扩大前奏。
- 保留 `grounded descending chorus`，这是唯一值得留下的旋律方向控制。
- 删除 bar count、mix essay、density map 等低吸收率指令。
- A 是唯一版本；无 B/C。

## 2. Vocal tags — minimal

推荐：保留极短 section tags，并把唯一总制作说明放在 Lyrics 外。

**Production note：**

```text
Immediate vocal start; brief natural breaths; no extra section repeats or extended instrumental passages.
```

Lyrics 内只使用：

- `[Cold Open: immediate close-mic male]`
- `[Verse 1: low conversational melody]`
- `[Pre-Chorus: subtle lift]`
- `[Chorus: grounded descending phrase]`
- `[Instrumental Breath]`
- `[Verse 2: intimate and sparse]`
- `[Short Refrain: restrained]`
- `[Bridge: stripped, near-spoken]`
- `[Brief Silence]`
- `[Instrumental Turnaround]`
- `[Final Chorus: grounded, controlled]`
- `[Outro: close male, distant female wordless hum]`

不再写 two-bar、half-bar、one-bar。那些不是 Suno 的可靠时间轴 API。

## 3. Opening captivation — surgical ONLY if worth it

值得做，但不新增歌词。

外部双审的共同有效信号不是“再写一句漂亮开场”，而是：陌生人应该先听见人物困境，再听建筑数据。最小手术是把现有 passenger couplet 移到最前面，并取消独立 Intro breath。总词数不增加，反而更早进入歌曲。

**Before：**

```text
Sixteen floors. Thirty-two doors on this floor alone.
Neighbors close enough to share a wall — far enough to never knock.
I put my socks on. I open the door.
```

**After：**

```text
I was living like a passenger
in a body I forgot how to drive —
floating two inches above the ride.
```

原来的三句不删除，紧接其后并入 Verse 1。原 Chorus 中的 passenger couplet相应移除，所以没有重复或扩写。

不采用：

- `I know every crack in this ceiling.`——新增、偏通用、降低田野特异性；
- Bridge car question 前置——会破坏 Bridge 首次明确提问；
- `heel first, then the whole foot`——增加词量，并把已经成立的 hook 解释得太满。

### Full revised LYRICS block

```text
[Cold Open: immediate close-mic male]

I was living like a passenger
in a body I forgot how to drive —
floating two inches above the ride.

[Verse 1: low conversational melody]

Sixteen floors. Thirty-two doors on this floor alone.
Neighbors close enough to share a wall — far enough to never knock.
I put my socks on. I open the door.

Frosted slab. Old bluestone that won't lie flat.
River-round cobble that rolls under the sole.
I slow like a kid and read the street with the feet —
three different weights, three different truths in the bone.
Cranes bang near and far at the same thin second —
the sound has weight; the phone in my pocket has none.

[Pre-Chorus: subtle lift]

My head can run the whole day clean and never leave the room,
reading the world from somewhere just above the wound.
The glass stays flat under the thumb. The street doesn't.
It asks for skin. It asks for time.

[Chorus: grounded descending phrase]

Quiet number eleven — not the fire, not the rush —
So I put my weight down.

[Brief Rest]

I put my weight down.
Stone under the shoe. Glass in the hand.
I came out of the screen to feel where the bluestone breaks.

[Instrumental Breath]

[Verse 2: intimate and sparse]

Two dogs at a kitchen door — grey-black, dirty white —
oil-smoke in the fur, already against my legs.
Not pretty. Not soft. They press me anyway.

Grandma at the stop: which bus, which when?
I open the little program I never need.
A small kindness moves. My clever mouth goes quiet.

[Short Refrain: restrained]

So I put my weight down.

[Brief Rest]

I put my weight down.
I came out of the screen to feel where the cobble rolls.

[Bridge: stripped, near-spoken]

Ten square meters. Blinds down. Headphones on.
Chasing the next bright thing till the body felt
gone.
Am I living in the skin —
or is the body just a car
for a brain that only trusts what lights up far?

[Brief Silence]

I set the phone face-down on the floor.
Then I put my socks on. Then I opened the door.

[Instrumental Turnaround]

[Final Chorus: grounded, controlled]

Quiet number eleven — trouble I dragged here still waits outside.
Still close, still far. Still glass in the pocket, stone under the stride.
So I put my weight down.

[Brief Rest]

I put my weight down.
Not cured. Not pure. The main thing stays unsolved.
Socks on. Door ajar. Feet on the stone of the world.

[Outro: close male, distant female wordless hum]

One more block with weight.
Not floating.
Not finished.

[End]
```

## 5. What NOT to do

- 不要再把 Profile、Lead、Female、Breath Plan 拼进 Style field。
- 不要 B/C，不要多 container 对照。
- 不要再写精确 two-bar、half-bar、one-bar 制作手册。
- 不要为了抓人新增 generic ceiling、healing 或 self-help 句。
- 不要把 Bridge car question 提前。
- 不要加入 `heel first`，也不要重写 weight hook。
- 不要重新加入 cicadas/butterfly。
- 不要再删 protect-list 核心。
- 不要用更多形容词解决 Suno 的时长问题。
- 不要开启 v5.3 density/polish loop。
