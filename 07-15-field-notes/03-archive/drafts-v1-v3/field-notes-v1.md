## Concept

Not a love song. Not a job song. A **field notebook** sung aloud:

A body that lived too long in screens walks Ningbo — Tianyi Pavilion river, Haishu slow streets, smart-park atom boxes, soft indoor wood court — and keeps writing what it sees. The city is **national #11 GDP and unnervingly quiet**. The narrator is half anthropologist, half someone trying to re-enter skin. Main problems stay unsolved. Intermittent depression eases when the body is placed back among living people and different stones underfoot.

**Unified axis:** soft contact / quiet surfaces — stone, wood, wind on skin, dogs against legs, close-and-far apartments. Not “fire city” energy. Not victory.

**What this song owns (not the 公子哥 song):** park ethnography, three floor textures, oil-smoke dogs, bus-stop grandma, quiet #11, Hangzhou-vs-Ningbo personification, atom apartment, soft basketball floor, hermit-screen loop → go outside.

I open the little program I never need.

Somewhere a courier coasts, not sprinting the street.

Maybe space is full enough that no one has to take.

Ten square meters. Headphones all day.

## Craft notes (v1)

**Hook:** “Quiet number eleven” — GDP rank as soft irony; singable, concrete, non-thesis.  
**Secondary hooks:** “Close and far” · “I put my body back outside” · “Gold-house girl next door.”  
**Show > tell:** wedding/fish/nets/three stones/dogs/grandma/soft floor carry the ethnography; only light naming of depersonalization via “floating,” “half ghost,” “light on glass.”  
**Bridge (3 moves):**  
1. Wound — hermit apartment loop  
2. Question — real vs screen / still ghost either room  
3. Act — socks, door, soft floor, living people  
**Ending:** no victory, no “I choose the city.” Problems remain; presence is partial.  
**Cuts for length:** typhoon, scooter, gym iron, 生财 counts, company basement — available for v2 if a section needs a new sample.  
**Avoided:** 公子哥 material; Naval name-drop; “Depersonalization” as clinical dump; Hangzhou/Ningbo as pure thesis without personification image.
